Susie's Chocolate Chip Cookies
================================

.. ingredients::

   - 2 cups flour (whole wheat medium grind)
   - 1 cup oats
   - 1/4 tsp. salt
   - 1/4 tsp. baking soda
   - 1/3 cup chocolate chips
   - just under 1/2 cup canola oil
   - 1/2 cup maple syrup
   - 1 tsp. vanilla
   - 2 tbsp. water

.. procedure::

   Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
   Mix the dry ingredients (flour, oats, salt, baking soda, chocolate chips).
   In a separate bowl, mix the oil, maple syrup, vanilla, and water.
   Put the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix.
   Shape, put on cookie sheet, and bake for 8 minutes.
