Sour Cream Banana Bread
========================

.. ingredients::

   - 1/2 cup butter
   - 1 cup sugar
   - 2 eggs, well beaten
   - 5-6 mashed overripe bananas
   - 1/2 cup sour cream
   - 1 tsp. baking soda
   - 2 cups flour

.. procedure::

   Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
   In a mixing bowl, beat butter and sugar until smooth and creamy.
   Add in eggs, bananas, sour cream, and baking soda.
   Stir in flour.
   Pour batter into an 8-inch greased loaf pan.
   Bake in pre-heated oven for 45 minutes to 1 hour.
