Hollandaise
=====================

.. ingredients::

   - 4 egg yolks
   - 1 Meyer lemon juice
   - pinch of salt
   - 1 stick of butter

.. procedure::

   Whisk the eggs and most of the lemon juice.
   On a double boiler, whisk while slowly streaming the melted butter in.
   Add the remaining lemon juice and salt to taste.
